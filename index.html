What We Do

We are a boutique wine consulting firm committed to curating luxury wine events, services, and merchandise that highlight black-owned brands in support of the black-owned wine industry. We offer private tastings, luxury wine boxes, and WINEtending services.

Try Our Public Wine Tasting

As a wine consulting firm, our ultimate goal for our wine tasting clients is to enhance their palate while exposing them to black-owned wine brands and educating them about the makings of wine.
